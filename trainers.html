We recognise the need to provide integrated people solutions to our Clients

Careers IT is a leading provider of business skills courses, designed for companies who want to train their staff as well as individuals who are seeking their own professional development. We offer courses in Computing, Project Management, Customer Care, Supervisory Skills, Business Writing Skills and much more.

Careers IT – An abridged profile

  • Established in 1994. Over 24 years of training experience
  • Accredited with LG Seta, MICT Seta and Services Seta
  • Microsoft Certified Partner

  • Level 1 BBBEE service provider
  • Successfully completed over 1 500 learnerships
  • Clients include government, parastatals, large corporates and SMME’s
  • Specialize in Business Skills and IT Skills development
  • Offer full qualifications from NQF level 1 to 6
